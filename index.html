[ MISSION_CORE ]

Standardizing Discipline through Evidence-Based Frameworks.

Our objective is to provide a rigorous operational environment where participants navigate global markets with clarity, prioritizing systemic risk mitigation over speculative outcomes.

01

Risk Per Trade

Define the maximum percentage of account equity you are prepared to risk on any single position. This value is set in advance and does not change based on emotion or "conviction."

02

Valid Setup

Enter only when a pre-defined setup is present and documented in your plan. No discretionary trades outside this plan are counted as valid performance.

03

Defined Exit

Set stop, target, and invalidation levels before entry. Respect the stop. Adjustments are made systematically, not reactively, and are recorded in the journal.

04

Position Sizing

Size positions mechanically based on distance to stop and chosen risk per trade. Avoid rounding up to "nice" sizes that increase real-world exposure.

05

Scenario Planning

Document key scenarios: what you will do if price moves for you, against you, or stalls. This reduces in-trade improvisation and emotional decision-making.

06

Post-Trade Review

After each trade, log the outcome, process compliance, and any deviations from the plan. Focus is on decision quality, not just net P&L.
